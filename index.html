Preface

Researches make wonders.

Unrecorded scientific research is not rigorous scientific research.


Although I am only a novice on the road of scientific research, I would like to record some of my own experience in this process, in order to provide you with some thinking and inspiration, and also give inspiration to myself in the future.

In this book, the content is not strictly limited, there may be mention of meta analysis, there may be mention of bioinformatics, it may even mention how to operate software on Linux, or some clinical surgical techniques.The span of information is very large, but I believe it is worth it. For a doctor, clinical work and scientific research are inseparable.Although the specific framework of the book has not yet been decided, the convenience of the Internet enables us to update and adjust the published content in a timely manner.

Hope this book can help you reduce detours.
